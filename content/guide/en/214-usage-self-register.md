# 2.4 AI large model official subscription top-up

> This article is provided by **CardNav [cardnav.xyz](https://cardnav.xyz)**.

Registering and topping up by yourself is the most troublesome path, but also the most controllable one. You need to handle overseas network, phone number verification, regional consistency, and payment channels yourself, but the account, email, payment method, and long-term usage records are all in your own hands.

## Core advantages

- **Long-term controllable**: The account, email, payment, and recovery methods all belong to you, with less dependence on migration and after-sales.
- **Clearer fund safety**: Bind your own payment method, without handing the payment step to an unfamiliar merchant.
- **Suitable for core use**: More suitable for long-term work, study, research, and personal knowledge base scenarios.

## Limitations and risks

- **High upfront barrier**: You need to prepare a stable network, clean IP, phone number, and usable payment channel.
- **High regional consistency requirement**: If registration, login, and payment regions differ too much, risk control may be triggered.
- **Official risk control still exists**: Registering yourself does not mean absolute safety; abnormal login, shared nodes, and payment anomalies may still cause problems.

## Suitable users

- People preparing for long-term heavy use of official accounts
- People whose accounts will accumulate important history, projects, or work materials
- People who do not want account assets and payment chains to depend on third-party merchants

## Practical steps

1. Prepare a clean and stable network proxy environment. First read [VPN and proxy access](./321-tool-vpn.md) and [IP purity check](./323-tool-ip-check.md).
2. Prepare an overseas phone number that can receive verification codes. For details, see [Overseas phone number verification](./324-tool-phone-verification.md).
3. Choose the payment method according to the target platform. You can first learn about [International payments and price differences](./330-payment-overview.md), [App Store payment](./332-payment-app-store.md), and [Google Play Store payment](./333-payment-google-play.md).
4. During registration, try to keep the network region, phone number region, payment region, and account information consistent.
5. After activating the subscription, do not frequently switch countries and nodes; also try to keep a stable environment in daily use.
6. Bind email, recovery methods, and secondary verification, and keep payment records to make it easier to handle later anomalies.

## Usage advice

If you have already confirmed that you will use a certain model long term, registering yourself is a path more worth investing in. Its cost is mainly in the upfront preparation, but once it works, later controllability is usually better than ready-made accounts and top-ups.
