# App Store payment

> This article is provided by **CardNav [cardnav.xyz](https://cardnav.xyz)**.

Paying through Apple's App Store is one of the most commonly used methods. Because App Store review and billing deduction are hosted by Apple officially, AI tools themselves, such as ChatGPT and Claude, cannot apply risk control to your actual payment card, so this is an excellent path for avoiding payment rejection and risk control.

The specific practical steps are as follows:

- **Prepare network and account**: Under a target-region network proxy (see [VPN and proxy access](./321-tool-vpn.md)), go to Apple's official website to register a brand-new Apple ID in the corresponding region.
- **Log in to the store and download**: Log in to the overseas Apple ID on an Apple device, enable the proxy, and download official App clients such as ChatGPT or Claude.
- **Top up App Store balance**: Purchase an App Store Gift Card for the corresponding region, redeem it in the app store, and top up the account balance.
- **The app triggers in-app purchase deduction**: Open the large model App and click subscribe, then use the account balance to complete payment in the App Store payment popup that is invoked.
